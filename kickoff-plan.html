The 12-Day Launch Plan

Once you activate the plan by paying the advance, here is the exact timeline we will follow to get your new business engine up and running.

Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-2)

We hit the ground running to get everything set up for success.

  • Kickoff Call Scheduled
  • Shared WhatsApp Group Created
  • Your Razorpay Account Setup Guided
  • Initial Assets (Logos, Content) Collected

Phase 2: Design & Build (Days 3-7)

Our team gets to work building your new digital storefront.

  • Website Homepage Design Draft (for your review)
  • Development of All Pages & Features
  • Admin Panel Setup

Phase 3: Review & Refine (Days 8-10)

We share our progress and get your valuable feedback.

  • Private Link to the Full Website for Your Review
  • One Round of Revisions Based on Your Feedback
  • Final Testing on All Devices (Mobile & Desktop)

Phase 4: Launch & Handoff (Days 11-12)

The exciting part! We go live and hand you the keys.

  • Your New Website is LIVE!
  • A short training video on how to use your Admin Panel.
  • Social Media Management officially begins.

What We'll Need From You (Your Role)

This is a partnership! To stick to this timeline, we'll just need a few things from you during Phase 1.

Photos & Text

Your best cake photos and the text for your "About Me" page.

Product Details

A list of your standard cakes with prices for the "Instant-Buy" shop.

Quick Feedback

Timely feedback during the "Review" phase to keep the project moving.
